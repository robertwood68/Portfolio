About Me

Hello! I'm Robert Wood.

I'm from York New Salem, PA, and I am 21 years old. Currently, I am attending York College of Pennsylvania as a commuter, and I am majoring in Computer Science. Having begun my college career as a Bio-Chem major, I soon realized that it was not the right field for me. Thankfully, this realization was caused by a lack of interest in the topics rather than my ability to understand the material. Having no prior experience programming, or even doing more than just small tech repairs for my personal devices, I decided to pursue computer science. Since taking the first step towards obtaining my B.S. in computer science, I haven't considered going into a different field, and I plan on getting my degree and attending graduate school to obtain my doctorate.
